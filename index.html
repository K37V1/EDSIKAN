Our mission is to provide varied but integrated civil engineering, WASH and research support services and capacity building to communities,
development organizations (both government and Non-governmental).

The Services We Provide

United for Real Development

ALL SERVICES

• We partner development organisations in providing civil engineering support, WASH services including capacity building and research. • We support organizations and communities in strategic planning and programme management for change. • We conduct training needs assessment and design training modules for organizations and communities. • We provide capacity building services to Metropolitan, Municipal and District Assemblies (MMDAs), NGOs etc. in strategic planning and management, Human Resources Management, Leadership, Project Management, Financial Management, Advocacy, grants management and partnership management, proposal development, Monitoring and Evaluation, procurement management, report writing, gender analysis. • We provide technical support in participatory program planning, implementation, monitoring and evaluation. • We conduct different forms of program/project baseline studies, impact evaluations and action research. • We facilitate the building of partnerships, negotiations and signing of MoUs among development agencies. • We provide technical support for communities in negotiating for compensation and signing of Social Responsibility Agreements in natural resource management. • We facilitate multi-stakeholder forums, workshops and seminars. • We provide awareness creation on topical development issues such as Climate Change, water resources governance and human rights. • We support communities in resource (environmental, human, cultural etc.) mobilization for local economic development.

BACKGROUND

EDSIKAN Co. Ltd. was incorporated as a limited liability firm under the Companies Act 2019 (Act 992) with registration number CS161701022 to provide Civil Engineering, Water, Sanitation and Hygiene Services and capacity building training and research. The motivation for the establishment of EDSIKAN is to augment the efforts of government and development agencies in their bid to maximize return on investment of development programmes and projects in Ghana and abroad. EDSIKAN is firmly rooted in the belief that development is a process and must be interdisciplinary and adopts an expertise-based practice-oriented approach to development solutions. The wide range of knowledge and experiences of EDSIKAN’s Associates in development work and research at all levels gives the organization the unique ability to mobilizing individuals, communities, policy makers and development partners to effect change that assures maximum benefit for all.
